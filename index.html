MAKING FOOTBALL TRULY GLOBAL: THE 2020-2023 VISION

This document contains a plan to further modernize the world of football, make it increasingly inclusive and pave the way for a landscape in which we will one day have around 50 national teams and 50 clubs from all continents at the highest competitive level. This is a vision to make football truly global.
